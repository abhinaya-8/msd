    Help & Support

    If you're having trouble using the Community Skill Sharing platform, check out these frequently asked questions or contact our support team.

    Frequently Asked Questions

    • How do I register as a worker?
      Click "Login" → Select "Work" → Fill in your skill details.
    • How can I hire someone?
      Click "Login" → Choose "Hire" → Browse available workers and send requests.
    • How to reset password?
      Use the "Forgot Password" link on the login page.
